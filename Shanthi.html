"Very little is needed to make a happy life; it is all within yourself, in your way of thinking." ~ Marcus Aurelius Antoninus

The Meaning of the Quote: This quote is similar to the first quote that was posted about the connection between having a positive thinking and happiness in life. This quote is reinforcing this concept which should be brought about in our daily lives. Tha amount of happiness and satisfaction we get at the end of each day is dependent on the way we control our thoughts. Maintaining our thoughts on positive events and all things positive, at the end of that dya, we will be taken over by positivity which will make you feel happiness. The more hapinness in life, the more enjoyable and better life becomes and we appreciate everything that comes along in life more. Though it may take time to avoid thinking in the wrong direction, start by keeping your mind in a close relationship with looking at positive things that makes the mind smile. Obviously it is imposiible to completly void certain thoughts, but our goal is to keep at the least, the majority of the thoughts positive. And like for everything in life, always keep trying and working hard towards your goal.
